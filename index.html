Mission Statement

  1. To offer comprehensive courses of study, training, and research in Health Technology, empowering students to achieve certificates, National and Higher National Diploma certificates, and become highly skilled professionals in the field.
  2. To equip individuals with the necessary knowledge and skills to serve as middle-level manpower in the health sector, meeting the growing demand for qualified healthcare professionals and contributing to the overall improvement of healthcare delivery.
  3. To provide a platform for the retraining of health workers, enabling them to update their skills, stay abreast of advancements in the healthcare industry, and enhance their career prospects.
  4. To create opportunities for lifelong learning and continuing education in various health and healthcare-related fields, ensuring professionals have access to the latest knowledge, practices, and advancements to deliver quality care and contribute to the overall well-being of individuals and communities.
